User Reviews

 
Decent attempt but no WOW here
25 June 2015 | by jackmeat (chicago) – See all my reviews

My quick rating - 5,7/10. Obvious tribute to the Tarantino style of thriller. Intertwining stories told out of order...you get the idea. Anyway, very nice to look at, the cinematography is the highlight of this one. The story mashes together fairly well, but never really pulls you in with a big WOW moment. Oh well, amusing flick, brutal when needed but not over the top, all and all, a good flick to watch if you like this type of movie and/or a fan of Simon Pegg. Even with the attempt to confuse with being non-linear I doubt most viewers if any will get lost. Oh, the soundtrack is extremely annoying and very repetitive, don't say I didn't warn you.
