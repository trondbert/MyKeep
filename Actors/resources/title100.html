This is on Disney Channel!?
26 January 2015 | by monsterfas (United States) – See all my reviews

I had no expectations for this movie. As soon as Zendaya was confirmed to star in it, I zoned out. She is a good dancer, but she overacts and she is IMO a terrible actor. Shake It up was terrible, and Zendaya was by far one of the worst parts. This show is no different. Its dumb and unfunny. I remember when Disney channel was all "Lilo and Stitch", and "Emperor's new Grove"..you know, actually Disney!! I got through like half of this before I turned it off from the bad jokes, and cheesiness. Also they make it so obvious that its a laugh machine that plays. Everything 'K.C.' said has a laugh in the background even if she said something like "WHAAA", in a stereotypical black way. The parents are terrible and just resemble irresponsible parents from another Disney Channel show *Cough* Shake It up *cough*. The brother is a terrible actor. The little girl made me face palm so many times. Disney Channel you are starting to seem Sexist. Name 1 time in the last 5-9 yrs there was a Male live action main character. Any? Of course not.
